"Go, therefore, and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, until the end of the age.” Matthew 28: 19-20

Meet Our Team:

Volunteers: Thank you to all our wonderful volunteers, we could not do this work without you. Volunteer biographies are forthcoming.


Founder: Joshua Correa
Biography: Joshua was born in Amarillo. He is a graduate of Holy Cross Catholic Academy. He attended Amarillo College and West Texas A&M University. Joshua has a B.S. in Biology and an M.S. in CIS. Joshua and his beautiful wife met on a Catholic dating website. Joshua, his wife, and children are active parishoners at St. Mary's Cathedral. Mrs. Correa coordinates music for the noon Mass and Joshua is an altar server. Joshua will begin teaching A Catholic Approach to Technology and Faith and Science this fall.

Pastor: Father Joseph Sheen Paul
Biography: Father Joseph was born in San Antonio, Texas. He felt that he was called to the priesthood while on a high school retreat. Father Joseph was ordained in 2005. He has an M.A. in Philosophy, and a Doctorate in Sacred Theology. We are excited to announce that Fr. Joseph will also begin teaching apologetics, basic theology and philosophy, and Church history this fall. He is a wonderful shepherd that wants everyone to love Christ as much as he does. Father Joseph demonstrates this by the beautiful Masses that he celebrates, his wonderful homilies, and his willingness to administer Sacraments at any time of day.

Counselor: Sister Maria Reyna, O.P.
Biography: Sister Maria was born in St. Augustine, Florida. As a young girl, Sister Maria was always drawn to helping others. While attending high school she felt called to serve God and her brothers and sisters. Sister Maria was drawn to the Order of Preachers and she professed final vows in 2008. She has a B.A. in Philosophy, an M.A. in Clinical Mental Health Counseling and an M.A. in Theology and Christian Ministry. Sister Maria will begin teaching Christian Counseling this fall. Sister Maria has a deep love for serving those who truly need the love and healing, of her beloved Spouse Jesus Christ, King of the Universe.
